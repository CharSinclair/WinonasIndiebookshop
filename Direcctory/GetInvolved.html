    Sponsor a Permanent Resident

    Make a huge impact on our rescue by sponsoring a sanctuary animal! Be a part of an exotic animal's story, support your favorite fox organization, and get a unique opportunity to invest in an individual’s life!
    Your sponsorship helps cover essential costs for the animals, including food, parasite prevention, bedding, cleaning, and enrichment. Extra donations are saved for medical emergencies or special projects.
    Thanks to you, these animals enjoy the best care possible!

    Sponsorship Information

    Each resident animal can have one Primary Sponsor and up to three Co-Sponsors. We ask all sponsors to commit to a 6-month term, as sponsorships provide our only reliable source of income and help us plan for the animals’ ongoing care.

    Donation Details:

    • Sponsorship donations are due between the 1st–5th of each month.
    • Donations must be set up as a recurring monthly payment via PayPal.
    • Once you submit the sponsorship form, we’ll contact you when a spot becomes available and provide a link to set up your donation.

    As a sponsor, you:

    • Receive updates on major medical or life events related to your sponsored resident.
    • Are mentioned in Instagram posts featuring your resident (if you provide your handle and opt-in).
    • Can schedule a private 30–60 minute tour of our facility. (Note: State regulations prohibit physical contact with the animals.)
